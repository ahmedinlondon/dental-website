Frequently Asked Questions

Orthodontic treatment is a carefully planned journey designed to create healthy, confident smiles at any age. At our clinic, we provide comprehensive consultations using digital scans, X-rays, and clinical photography to develop fully personalised treatment plans. We offer a full range of modern orthodontic solutions, including traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, lingual braces, and clear aligner therapy for discreet treatment. For growing patients, functional appliances may be used to guide jaw development, while adults benefit from aesthetic and minimally visible options. Throughout treatment, we provide regular monitoring, adjustments, and emergency care when needed. After treatment is complete, we ensure long-term stability with custom retainers and follow-up care, helping you maintain your results for years to come.
