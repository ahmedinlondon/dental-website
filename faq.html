Frequently Asked Questions

Orthodontic treatment is a carefully planned journey designed to create healthy, confident smiles at any age. We offer comprehensive assessments, modern treatment options including braces and clear aligners, and full post-treatment care to ensure long-lasting results.

An orthodontist is a dentist with additional specialist training focused on tooth movement and jaw alignment.

No, orthodontic treatment can be successful at any age. Many adults choose discreet options like aligners.

Most treatments last between 12 and 30 months depending on complexity.

Aligners are effective for mild to moderate cases, while braces are best for complex treatments.

Aligners are generally more comfortable as they are smooth and removable, while braces may cause temporary irritation.

Retainers keep your teeth in their new position and prevent them from shifting back.
